# Custom Algorithms

Take control of your feed with custom algorithms.

## What Are Custom Algorithms?

Instead of corporate algorithms controlling what you see, create your own rules for content discovery.

## Creating Custom Algorithms

**Coming Soon**: This feature is under development.

Custom algorithms will let you:
- Prioritize content from specific users
- Filter by keywords and topics
- Weight different types of engagement
- Create time-based filters
- Build recommendation systems

## Use Cases

- Focus on educational content
- Filter out certain topics
- Prioritize close friends
- Discover new voices
- Curate niche interests
