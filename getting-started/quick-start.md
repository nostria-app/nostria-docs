# Quick Start Guide

Get started with Nostria in just a few minutes! This guide will walk you through the essential steps to join the decentralized social media revolution.

## Step 1: Access Nostria

Choose your preferred platform:

- **Web App**: Visit [nostria.app](https://nostria.app) in your web browser
- **Desktop App**: Download from the [Microsoft Store](https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/9n7f0twq0d8g) or [GitHub releases](https://github.com/nostria-app/nostria/releases)
- **Mobile**: Coming soon to iOS and Android app stores

For most users, starting with the web app is the quickest option.

## Step 2: Create Your Account

Creating a Nostria account is incredibly simple:

1. Click the **"Sign Up"** or **"Get Started"** button
2. Your cryptographic keys will be automatically generated
3. That's it! No email, no phone number, no verification required

## Step 3: Set Up Your Profile

Make your profile uniquely yours:

1. Click on your profile icon or settings
2. Add a profile picture (avatar)
3. Write a bio to introduce yourself
4. Add a display name
5. Optionally add a banner image, website, or other links

Don't worry‚Äîyou can always update this later!

## Step 4: Find People to Follow

Build your social network:

1. **Explore the Global Feed**: See what everyone is talking about
2. **Use the Search**: Look for topics or people that interest you
3. **Check Recommended Users**: Nostria suggests interesting accounts
4. **Import from Other Platforms**: If you have contacts on other Nostr clients

Click "Follow" on anyone interesting to see their posts in your feed.

## Step 5: Make Your First Post

Share something with the world:

1. Click the **"Create Post"** or **"What's on your mind?"** button
2. Type your message (up to several thousand characters)
3. Optionally add images, videos, or links
4. Click **"Post"** to share

Congratulations! You're now part of the decentralized social network! üéâ

## Step 6: Explore Key Features

Now that you're set up, explore:

- **Feed**: Your personalized timeline of posts from people you follow
- **Notifications**: See who liked, replied, or mentioned you
- **Messages**: Send private direct messages to other users
- **Communities**: Join groups around your interests
- **Settings**: Customize your experience

## Quick Tips for New Users

### Do's ‚úÖ
