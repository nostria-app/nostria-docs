## How It Works

Nostria connects to the Nostr network using:

- **Your identity**: A cryptographic key pair that proves you are you
- **Relays**: Servers that store and forward your messages
- **Clients**: Apps like Nostria that provide the user interface

When you post on Nostria, your message is signed with your private key and sent to relays. Other users can then retrieve your posts from these relays. It's like emailâ€”decentralized and interoperable.
