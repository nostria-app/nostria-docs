## Add to Home Screen (Mobile)

Use Nostria like a native app on your phone:

### On iOS (Safari)
1. Open [nostria.app](https://nostria.app) in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Name it "Nostria" and tap "Add"

### On Android (Chrome)
1. Open [nostria.app](https://nostria.app) in Chrome
2. Tap the menu (three dots)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Name it "Nostria" and tap "Add"

Now you have a Nostria icon on your home screen!

## Troubleshooting

### Page won't load
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page (F5 / Cmd+R)
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

### Features not working
- Update your browser to the latest version
- Disable browser extensions that might interfere
- Check browser console for errors (F12)

### Performance issues
- Close other tabs and applications
- Clear browser cache
- Disable unnecessary browser extensions
- Check your internet speed
