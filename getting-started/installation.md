## Multi-Device Usage

The beauty of Nostria is that you can use it on multiple devices simultaneously:

1. **Use the same account everywhere**: Your cryptographic keys work across all platforms
2. **Sync seamlessly**: Your profile and posts are available on all devices
3. **Stay connected**: Check notifications on mobile, post from desktop, browse on web

### Setting Up Multi-Device

1. Install Nostria on your first device and create your account
2. **Safely save your private key** (nsec)
3. Install Nostria on additional devices
4. **Import your private key** to log in with the same account
5. Enjoy synchronized access!
