# Your First Steps

Congratulations on creating your Nostria account! Let's get you started with the essentials to make the most of your decentralized social experience.

## Step 1: Secure Your Keys (If You Haven't Already)

Before doing anything else:

1. **Save your private key** in a secure location
2. **Test your backup** by logging out and back in
3. **Verify it works** before proceeding

See [Creating Your Account](creating-account.md#critical-save-your-private-key) for detailed instructions.

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile

Make your profile stand out:

### Add a Profile Picture
1. Click your profile icon
2. Select "Edit Profile"
3. Click the avatar area
4. Upload a photo (JPG, PNG, max 5MB)
5. Crop if desired
6. Save

### Write Your Bio
1. In Edit Profile, find the bio field
2. Write 2-3 sentences about yourself
3. Include interests, profession, or fun facts
4. Use emojis to add personality üòä

### Add Other Details
- **Display Name**: Your preferred name (can be anything)
- **Banner**: Large header image for your profile
- **Website**: Your blog, portfolio, or other links
- **Lightning Address**: For receiving tips (optional)
- **NIP-05 Identifier**: Verification badge (optional, see below)

[Learn more about profile customization ‚Üí](../features/profile-setup.md)

## Step 3: Find People to Follow

An empty feed is boring! Build your network:

### Explore the Global Feed
1. Click **"Global"** or **"Explore"**
2. Browse recent posts from everyone
3. Find interesting people
4. Click **"Follow"** on users you like

### Use Search
1. Click the search icon
2. Search for:
   - Topics you're interested in (#photography, #bitcoin, etc.)
   - Names of people you know
   - Companies or projects
3. Follow accounts that interest you

### Check Recommendations
1. Go to **"Discover"** or **"Suggestions"**
2. Nostria suggests popular or relevant users
3. Follow a few to get started

### Import from Other Platforms
If you're coming from other Nostr clients:
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Import/Export
2. Import your follow list
3. Your social graph transfers over!

**Tip**: Start by following 10-20 people to build your feed.

Make Nostria work for you:

1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Notifications**

## Tips for New Users

### Do's ‚úÖ
- **Be authentic**: Share what genuinely interests you
- **Engage regularly**: Comment, like, and participate
- **Be respectful**: Treat others as you'd like to be treated
- **Ask questions**: The community is helpful!
- **Explore features**: Try everything Nostria offers
- **Back up your key**: Seriously, do it again to be sure

### Don'ts ‚ùå
- **Don't spam**: Quality over quantity
- **Don't share your private key**: Never, with anyone
- **Don't expect overnight success**: Building a following takes time
- **Don't feed trolls**: Use block/mute features
- **Don't forget to have fun**: It's social media, enjoy it!

## Understanding Your Feed

### Home Feed
- Posts from people you follow
- Chronological order (usually)
- Your main timeline

### Global Feed
- Posts from everyone on the network
- Great for discovery
- Can be chaotic but interesting

### Notifications
- Activity related to you
- Check regularly to stay engaged
- Respond to build connections

## Building Your Network

Start small and grow organically:

### Week 1
- Follow 20-30 people
- Post 2-3 times
- Engage with 5-10 posts daily

### Week 2
- Explore communities
- Try different post types
- Interact more consistently

### Month 1
- Develop your voice
- Find your niche
- Build genuine connections

## Common First-Day Questions

**Q: Why is my feed empty?**
A: Follow more people! Your feed shows posts from people you follow.

**Q: Can I delete posts?**
A: Yes, though they may remain on some relays due to decentralization.

**Q: How do I get more followers?**
A: Post quality content, engage with others, be active, and give it time.

**Q: What should I post about?**
A: Anything! Your interests, thoughts, questions, photos, links, etc.

**Q: Is Nostria like Twitter/X?**
A: Similar, but decentralized, privacy-focused, and you own your data.
