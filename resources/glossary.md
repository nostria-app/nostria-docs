# Glossary

Key terms and concepts explained.

## Nostr Terms

**Nostr**: Notes and Other Stuff Transmitted by Relays - the protocol

**NIP**: Nostr Implementation Possibility - protocol standards

**Relay**: Server that stores and forwards Nostr messages

**Client**: Application for accessing Nostr (like Nostria)

**Event**: Any action or message on Nostr

**Kind**: Type of event (1=post, 4=DM, etc.)

## Keys & Identity

**Public Key (npub)**: Your identifier, safe to share

**Private Key (nsec)**: Your password, never share

**Keypair**: Public + private key together

**NIP-05**: DNS-based verification identifier

**Lightning Address**: Payment identifier for tips

## Content

**Post/Note**: Public message on Nostr

**DM**: Direct message, encrypted

**Repost**: Sharing someone's post

**Quote**: Repost with added commentary

**Reaction**: Like or emoji response

**Zap**: Bitcoin tip via Lightning

## Social

**Follow**: Subscribe to someone's posts

**Follower**: Someone who follows you

**Mention**: Tagging someone in a post

**Hashtag**: Topical label (#topic)

**Community**: Group around shared interest

**Feed**: Stream of posts

## Technical

**WebSocket**: Connection protocol for relays

**Encryption**: Securing messages

**Signature**: Cryptographic proof of authorship

**Decentralization**: No central authority

**Interoperability**: Working across different apps
